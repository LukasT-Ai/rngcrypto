## Why L2s Matter

Layer 2 networks solve Ethereum's scaling trilemma without sacrificing security. In 2026, L2s collectively process more transactions than Ethereum mainnet, and the ecosystem is maturing fast.

## The Top 5

### 1. Arbitrum (ARB)

The king of optimistic rollups. Arbitrum processes 40% of all L2 transactions and hosts the largest DeFi ecosystem outside of mainnet.

- **TVL:** $14.2B
- **Daily TX:** 2.1M
- **Key protocols:** GMX, Aave, Uniswap, Pendle

### 2. Base

Coinbase's L2 has become the default onboarding ramp for new crypto users. Zero gas fees for most interactions and seamless Coinbase wallet integration.

- **TVL:** $8.7B
- **Daily TX:** 3.4M (highest volume)
- **Key protocols:** Aerodrome, Moonwell, Extra Finance

### 3. Optimism (OP)

The Superchain vision is playing out. Multiple chains built on the OP Stack are interoperable, creating a network effect.

- **TVL:** $6.8B
- **Daily TX:** 1.8M
- **Key protocols:** Velodrome, Synthetix, Aave

### 4. zkSync Era

The leading zk-rollup. Faster finality than optimistic rollups, and the tech is finally production-ready.

- **TVL:** $3.2B
- **Daily TX:** 900K
- **Key protocols:** SyncSwap, Maverick, ZeroLend

### 5. Starknet

The dark horse. StarkWare's STARK proofs offer the strongest cryptographic guarantees, and the developer tooling has improved dramatically.

- **TVL:** $1.8B
- **Daily TX:** 450K
- **Key protocols:** JediSwap, Ekubo, Nostra

## Investment Thesis

L2 tokens are undervalued relative to their usage metrics. The market is still pricing them as "Ethereum competitors" when they're actually "Ethereum amplifiers."
